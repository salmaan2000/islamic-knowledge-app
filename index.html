About Islam

Islam began in the 7th century CE in Mecca with the revelation to Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him). Key aspects include:

History:

  • Revelation of Quran over 23 years
  • Spread of Islam from Arabia to worldwide
  • Major historical periods and dynasties

Practices:

  • Five daily prayers (Salah)
  • Annual fasting in Ramadan
  • Obligatory charity (Zakat)
  • Pilgrimage to Mecca (Hajj)
  • Declaration of faith (Shahada)

Quran Verses

Important verses with translations and contexts:

  • Al-Fatiha (1:1-7): Opening chapter of the Quran
    Translation: "In the name of Allah, Most Gracious, Most Merciful..."
    Context: Recited in every prayer, summarizes Islamic teachings
  • Ayat Al-Kursi (2:255): The Throne Verse
    Translation: "Allah - there is no deity except Him, the Ever-Living..."
    Context: Powerful verse about Allah's attributes
  • Surah Al-Ikhlas (112:1-4)
    Translation: "Say, He is Allah, [who is] One..."
    Context: Describes pure monotheism
